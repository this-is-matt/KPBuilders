Utah Construction You Can Trust

KP Builders brings decades of excellence to construction projects in and around Northern Utah, from Ogden to Brigham City to Layton. Our founder, Kenny Palmer, started his building career while still in high school. Since then, he has earned a reputation for being trustworthy and respectful in all his ventures.
Since learning to build new homes and additions early in his career, Kenny has focused on various aspects of the construction trade, including framing and concrete, finish carpentry, outdoor signage and additions. Kenny takes the lead on each project to get it done right the first time. That means meeting your expectations, staying within your budget and ensuring that all the legal details and permits are in place.
Often, KP Builders hires subcontractors to help us complete jobs on time and on budget. As a fully licensed and insured construction management team, we do the research, analyze bids, vet company reputations and manage timelines to provide an end result you will love.
